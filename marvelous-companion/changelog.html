Changelog

Marvelous Companion

0.1.5

July 2026 · Internal Testing (Android & iOS)

  • Figures and their decks are now combined into a single Character view — browse a character and its cards in one place
  • Game sets now show their characters as a row of tappable cards that jump straight to each character’s details
  • Team characters (like A-Force) list their members the same way
  • Clearer filtering with a labeled chip that defaults to Characters
  • Redesigned details screen — all stats now live in a single card, set and season moved into a header pill, and tidier spacing
  • Fixed Hulkling’s missing Impersonating sidebar card, corrected villain cards for some dual characters, and cleaned up stray card data

0.1.4

July 2026 · Internal Testing (Android & iOS)

  • Batman Hush is now available, with new heroes, villains, and cards to explore
  • Hero card abilities now show action icons instead of spelled-out text, matching the physical cards
  • General text and formatting cleanup across hero cards
  • Fix software keyboard not showing when searching

0.1.3

July 2026 · Internal Testing (Android & iOS)

  • Brand new app icon
  • Refreshed design for the details screen, including an improved toggle for dual characters
  • Smoother, more predictable back gestures on newer Android versions
  • Under-the-hood updates to keep the app fast and current on the latest Android and iOS

0.1.2

July 2026 · Internal Testing (Android & iOS)

  • Marvelous Companion is now available on iOS via TestFlight, with the same features as the Android version
  • About screen now displays the current app version
  • New feedback button — tapping it copies your version to clipboard and opens the feedback form
  • Support email is now a tappable link on the About screen

0.1.1

June 2026 · Internal Testing

  • Added app launcher icon
  • Updated support contact to support@punkforge.dev

0.1.0

June 2026 · Internal Testing

  • Browse Game Sets

  • Browse Figures and Components (decks)
